               $f'(r)=\var{c}-\sqrt{r}$. How many units can be produced if 
               \var{n} additional labour is used?\\$S=$\ansref}}
               \begin{answer}
                    \solution{sol}
               \end{answer}
               \lang{de}{\explanation{Mit Hilfe des Integrals $\int_0^{\var{n}}(\var{c}-\sqrt{r})dr$ kann die

               \lang{en}{\explanation{The additional number of pieces produced can be
